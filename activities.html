Our hayrides are a very popular attraction. Feel free to engage on a single or group ticket though the farm and learn about the work we do.

Head out to our pumkin path have some fun by picking the right one for you and getting creative. You have the option to buy them as well.

Why not head out and pick your own produce from our farm. Going solo or as a family it's sure going to be fun finding and creating your own fruit basket.

Head out to the field and visit our farm animals. They like pictures as long as you ask them nicely. You will find sheeps, cows, chickens and lots more!.
